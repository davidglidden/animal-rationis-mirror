Playing

Just returned from Cremona—Monteverdi’s Vespers with Jordi Savall, Le Concert des Nations, and La Capella Reial de Catalunya. The heart of violin country, where even the stones remember Stradivari. That final Amen—improvised, eternal. Read the full reflection.

Preparing for Vienna, 20–26 May: Beethoven Symphonies 1, 2, 4, 8, and 9 at the Wiener Konzerthaus. The shift from Monteverdi’s divine architecture to Beethoven’s human struggle—this is why I love this work.
